Physiotherapy is one of the medical health care professions, which supports people to maintain their health, improve movement and enhance their strength and flexibility. A physiotherapist has deep knowledge of the body, diagnose and treat symptoms of illness, injury or any disability.

A physiotherapist can help people from birth to death treating simple back pain to neurological disorders.
