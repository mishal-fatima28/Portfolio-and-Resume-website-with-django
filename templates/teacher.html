What is Teacher Training, it's significance and how i can help you in this regard?

A systemic development program involving a new teacher or ongoing in-service person by polishing his knowledge and skills towards its teaching style is ‘Teacher Training’. A person will teach what he learns and what he can express so ‘Teacher Training’ refers to an enhancement of skills and abilities of teachers to help them better deal with students. This includes sessions or guidance programs other than formal education with the purpose of training teachers to complete classroom tasks effectively.

Significance of Teacher Training

The teacher plays a key role in the development of student personality through a learning process. If the teacher is not completely competent, student growth will get affected. So it is important to allow proper teacher training as it improves the proficiency and knowledge level of teachers towards its class. Training systems allow a teacher to create new strategies and teaching skills through the provision of a framework to engage students in-class learning. The teacher must be trained properly before entering the classroom or during the ongoing teaching process to avoid the risk of proficiency problems.
